# VeriNews Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

VeriNews is an AI-powered platform designed to help people verify online news and detect misinformation quickly and reliably.

The brand focuses on improving information transparency and empowering users to evaluate the credibility of online content.

Core brand values:

Truth  
Transparency  
Trust  
Technology

Primary audience:

- Social media users
- General public

---

## Brand Mission

To provide accessible AI-powered tools that help people identify misinformation and verify online content easily.

---

## Brand Vision

To create a digital environment where misinformation is rapidly detected and accurate information becomes easier to verify.

---

## Brand Personality

The VeriNews brand should communicate the following characteristics:

Trustworthy  
Intelligent  
Transparent  
Modern  
Helpful

---

## Brand Voice

The tone of communication should be:

Clear  
Neutral  
Educational  
Fact-focused  

Example:

Correct tone:
“Analysis suggests this content may contain misleading information.”

Avoid aggressive statements such as:
“This news is completely fake.”

The platform should inform users rather than judge content.

---

## Logo Guidelines

The VeriNews logo should visually represent **verification and trustworthy news**.

Recommended visual elements include:

- Checkmark
- Shield
- Magnifying glass
- Document or newspaper icon

Logo rules:

- Maintain consistent spacing around the logo
- Do not stretch or distort the logo
- Only use official brand colors

---

## Color Palette

Primary Blue  
#2563EB

Alert Red  
#DC2626

Neutral Gray  
#6B7280

Dark Background  
#111827

Light Background  
#F9FAFB

These colors should be used consistently across the interface and branding materials.

---

## Typography

Primary font:

Inter

Fallback fonts:

Roboto  
Helvetica  
Arial

Text hierarchy:

H1 — Bold  
H2 — SemiBold  
Body — Regular

Typography should prioritize readability and clarity.

---

## UI Design Style

The interface design should be:

Minimal  
Clean  
Information-focused  
User-friendly  

Recommended UI components include:

- Prediction badges
- Confidence indicators
- Result explanation panels
- Submission forms for text, images, and URLs

---

## Result Labels

VeriNews predictions use two primary labels:

REAL  
Indicates the content likely contains accurate information.

FAKE  
Indicates the content likely contains misleading or false information.

Example output:

Prediction: FAKE  
Confidence: 87%

Confidence scores should always be displayed alongside predictions.

---

## Accessibility

The platform should support accessibility standards including:

- High color contrast
- Clear and readable typography
- Screen reader compatibility
- Mobile responsive design

Accessibility ensures that all users can interact with the platform effectively.

---

## Brand Do

- Provide transparent prediction explanations
- Display confidence scores
- Encourage users to verify information

---

## Brand Don't

- Present predictions as absolute truth
- Promote political bias
- Hide model uncertainty

---

## Future Brand Expansion

Future VeriNews products may include:

- Browser extension for real-time verification
- Mobile application
- Public misinformation monitoring dashboard
